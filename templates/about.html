About Resume Analyzer

Resume Analyzer is a smart web-based tool designed to help job seekers and recruiters gain quick insights from resumes. Whether you're preparing for your next job or screening candidates, this platform makes resume evaluation faster and easier.

What It Does:

  • Extracts key information from uploaded resumes
  • Identifies relevant skills automatically
  • Provides a clean summary of the resume content

Why Use Resume Analyzer?

Traditional resume screening can be time-consuming. Resume Analyzer leverages automation to reduce effort, helping you focus on decision-making rather than manual reading.

Built By:

This tool was developed by Mohanesh as a project to combine Python, Flask, HTML/CSS, and database integration. It's a continuously evolving platform with upcoming features like login, data storage, and analytics.

Future Enhancements:

  • Login and registration for users
  • Resume analysis history storage
  • Job-role matching based on skills

Thank you for visiting Resume Analyzer. Your feedback and suggestions are always welcome!
